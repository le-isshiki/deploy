Privacy Policy

SwitchCash is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

1. Information we collect

2. How we use your information

  • Process and complete your transfers
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Send transaction confirmations and account alerts
  • Comply with Haitian financial regulations and AML requirements
  • Improve our service and troubleshoot issues
  • Send service announcements (not marketing without consent)

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Mobile wallet providers (MonCash, NatCash) — to process transfers
  • Technology providers — Netlify (hosting), Neon (database), Resend (email). All are under data processing agreements.
  • Law enforcement — when legally required or to prevent fraud
  • Regulators — to comply with Haitian financial oversight requirements

4. Data retention

We retain transaction records for a minimum of 5 years as required by Haitian financial regulations. Account data is retained while your account is active plus 2 years after closure. You may request deletion of non-financial personal data (see Section 7).

5. Security

We protect your data using:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Bcrypt hashing for passwords (cost factor 12)
  • Database encryption at rest
  • Rate limiting and account lockout on authentication endpoints
  • Admin access logging and audit trails

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

6. Cookies & tracking

SwitchCash uses only essential session cookies for authentication. We do not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics cookies, or fingerprinting technologies.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account (subject to regulatory retention requirements)
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children

SwitchCash is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We will notify you by email before making significant changes to this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects when changes were last made.
